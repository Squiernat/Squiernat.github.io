How it works

Eater

#1 Search for available meals in your community

#3 Pick-up the meal, get to know your HomeCook, and enjoy the food

Home Cook

#1 Publish your dish with a picture, price, full list of ingredients and pick-up timeframe

#2 Be there to hand over your delicious creation and meet your community

#3 Receive your money via bank transfer or PayPal in the following days

Main Benefits

Saving money

By selling your delicious meals to the Hot Pot community, you can split the cost of your meal preparation.
All transactions are made through our secure app.
Afterwards, you can transfer your wallet money to your personal bank account or PayPal at any time
